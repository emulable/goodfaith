ü§î What Even Is Manipulation?

Manipulation isn't just "someone being mean to you." It's also not the same as coercion (where someone directly threatens you) or confusion (where things are just unclear).

Manipulation is when someone secretly influences your choices in ways you can't see, and makes it expensive to ask questions.

Two things have to happen at the same time:

üå´Ô∏è The Fog

You can't see clearly what's happening. Who's doing what? Why? What happens if you push back? Will anyone have your back? The answers are kept vague on purpose.

üöß The Fence
